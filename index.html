About Us

Commandos Football Academy is a football academy, to create a positive learning environment in which players are encouraged to express themselves and play freely without fear, and to nurture young players so that they can achieve their full potential. Located in Pune, the academy aims to brings international coaching and training facilities through a curriculum prepared under the guidance of our Technical Director Mr.Rocky Arokiaswamy.

Why Choose Us

Our Coaches are always upgrading their knowledge and develop a practice plan which is realistic to the onfield situation that helps them to achieve their session goals.

We always look for signing great contracts and useful tie-ups for the development of football and nurturing great talents at our academy.

We work hard to develop magical players who will represent our country and take football to the next level.

We only sponsor hardworking and extra talented players. Our sponsors appreciate investing in talented players.

We strongly believe that players should be always developed with all moral values and thought to be kind to every living being on earth.

We are always improving our methodologies and player development framework so that players can be developed Tactically, Technically, Physically and Mentally.
